About

This application helps you manage your campus life effectively.

Purpose

The purpose of this application is to provide students with a centralized platform to manage their academic and extracurricular activities, ensuring they stay organized and informed.

Key Features

Task/Event Management

Create, edit, and delete tasks and events with ease. Set reminders to stay on top of your schedule.

Regex Search

Utilize powerful regex search capabilities to find and manage your tasks and events efficiently.

Form Validation

Ensure that all forms are filled out correctly before submission.

Import/Export

Easily import and export your tasks and events to and from other platforms.

If you have any questions or feedback, feel free to reach out!
